Virat Kohli (Hindi: [ʋɪˈɾɑːʈ ˈkoːɦliː] ; born 5 November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the India national cricket team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time [ citation needed ] , Kohli plays as a right-handed batsman for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League and for Delhi in domestic Indian cricket.
